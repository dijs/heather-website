What I do

Graphic Design

There is a creativity and logic envolved in all marketing and graphic design. I create logos, infographics, web-layouts and much more.

Photography

I received a BFA in Photography and enjoy the subtle nuances of lighting, staging and producing. I contract out for portraits and events. My personal work has been shown in galleries such as 500x, the Roving museum of art and the Dallas Library.

Freelance Art

I dabble in many medias on top of photography and graphic design; including paint, wood, installation, charcoal, graphite and anything else I can get my hands on.

About

Heather van der Dys

  • The Beginning

    Not Such a Long Time Ago

    I have been an artist all of my life, like most. I played guitar, piano, harp, and trumpet throughout my childhood. I am 3-d in nature and dabble in many medias. The dirtier I can get my hands, the better.

  • 2011-2014

    College and Experience

    I graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Texas at Arlington with a Bachelors of Fine Arts and a concentration in Photography. I have been privileged to show my work in galleries like 500X, the Roving Museum of Art, and the Dallas Library. I also interned as an assistant photographer and graphic designer during my college experience.

  • 2015

    Full Time Graphic Designer

    Yes, for a church. It may be surprising, but it is a fast paced, multi-disciplined job full of ideas and opportunities for branding, art, and creativity.

  • More to Come

    Feel Free to Contact Me

    I am Married, have one 75lb puppy. Yeah, puppy. Love all animals, wear glasses, and still play music for fun. I contract every service shown in my portfolio. I hope you have enjoyed this little tour.
